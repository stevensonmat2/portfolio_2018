Rules:

Each game consists of three rounds. To progress, each players need a certain combination of dice

To pass Round One, a player needs a 1 and a 2; for Round Two, a 3 and 4; for Round 3, a 5 and 6

If a player rolls two 3's, they are sent back to Round One

Each time a player rolls a double, they fill the corresponding spot on their DoubleStrike column

If a player has every DoubleStrike filled, they may use the DoubleStrike ability to win the game automatically
